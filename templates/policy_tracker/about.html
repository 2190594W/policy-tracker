About

Policy Tracker is a website created by Benjamin Goodman, Christopher Atkinson, Christopher Watson and James Mushahwar for their Web App Development course at the University of Glasgow. The idea behind Policy Tracker is to allow citizens of a country to add their government's manifestos and monitor their governments progress, allowing citizens to hold their government accountable. Once a promise has been uploaded, citizens can update these election promises to track their governments progress during their electoral term by marking promises broken, completed, in-progress and not yet started.
