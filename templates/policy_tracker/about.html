About

Policy Tracker

Policy Tracker is a website created by Benjamin Goodman, Christopher Atkinson, Christopher Watson and James Mushahwar for their Web App Develpment course at the University of Glasgow. The idea behind Policy Trackr is to allow citizens of a country to add their goverments to account by adding the governments manifesto to Policy Tracker. Once uploaded citizens can update these election promises to track their governments progress during their electoral term by marking promises broken, completed, in-progress and not yet started.
