Freqently Asked Questions

What is Policy tracker?

The object of our application is to give citizens around the world a method of holding their government accountable for their campaign promises and allowing user discuss and rate the promises. The application will follow the open source model, allowing users to add their country's government to the website and subsequently add the promises and manifestos that government made during their electoral campaign. Upon adding these promises, users will be able to update them accordingly, allowing users to see whether their government has achieved or broken the promises they made during their electoral campaign.

Why did you create Policy Tracker?

Our application will give users the ability compile government electoral promises in one place, helping to prevent the common occurrence of a government removing their website with all their manifesto and promises in an attempt to make it harder to hold them accountable during their term.

How did you come up with this idea?

We were influenced by the Trudeaumeter. The Trudeaumeter tracks Justin Trudeau's electoral promises while maintaining no political affiliations. The site enables Canadians to hold Justin Trudeau's government accountable for their manifesto and measure what the government has achieved during its term in office.
